How DVVIA works

Trust is built in,
not promised.

Every step of buying or selling on DVVIA is designed so there's no logical way to cheat.

01
🪪
Sellers verify their identity
Before posting anything, sellers submit a government ID and USPS mail for review. We confirm their real name and address. Anonymous sellers don't exist on DVVIA.
02
📄
We review the title
Sellers submit a photo of the vehicle title before listing. Our team checks ownership and lien status before the car appears on DVVIA. No title, no listing.
03
📍
We choose where you meet
The seller cannot pick the meeting location — we do, based on their verified address. It's always a public place. The seller cannot ask you to go anywhere else.
04
🤝
You meet, inspect, and decide
Both sides check in on arrival through the app. Everything is accountable. No-shows are flagged. The deal happens in person, in public, on your terms.
Why DVVIA is different

Not just safer.
Structurally safer.

🚫
No fake listings
Every listing is reviewed by our team before going live. We check the title, VIN, and photos. If something doesn't add up, it never gets published.
vs. Craigslist: anyone can post anything
🪪
No anonymous sellers
Every seller's real identity is confirmed with government ID and verified mail before they can post. You know exactly who you're dealing with — even if you don't see their name.
vs. Facebook: a profile photo isn't verification
📍
No dangerous meetups
The meeting location is set by DVVIA, not the seller. It's always a public place near the seller's verified address. They cannot change it or pressure you to go elsewhere.
vs. everywhere else: meet at my house at 9pm
📝
No misleading descriptions
Sellers can't write free-text descriptions. Everything on a listing came from documents, photos, and tap-only condition questions — not seller claims.
vs. everywhere else: "runs great, no issues"
